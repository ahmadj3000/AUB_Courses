About AUB Reviews

AUB Reviews is a student-driven academic platform created as part of the CMPS 271 Software Engineering Project at the American University of Beirut. It is designed to bridge the gap between students' real experiences and the static nature of traditional course descriptions. By empowering students to share honest feedback on course content, workload, difficulty, and teaching quality, AUB Reviews fosters transparency and informed academic planning.

The platform also encourages collaborative learning by enabling the sharing of study materials and class notes. At its core, AUB Reviews is built on a philosophy of peer support, digital innovation, and academic integrity. It strives to become a dynamic ecosystem where students can reflect on their learning journeys, support each other’s success, and contribute to an ever-growing, student-centered academic archive.

Developed by a dedicated team of AUB students, this project exemplifies how applied software engineering can meet real campus needs while nurturing a culture of openness, respect, and continuous improvement.
